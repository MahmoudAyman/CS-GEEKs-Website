Build Nights

Photoes from Robotics Build Night.
At CS GEEKs venue at Nile University.

Haven't heard about Buildnights? Well, a build night is a one-day "night" project where we meet to learn how to finalize our project. The idea is to learn new skills and make cool projects in a funny and engaging way, and as you can infer from the title; everything in one night.
